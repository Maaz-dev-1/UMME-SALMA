To My Extraordinary Teacher

💌 A Letter from My Heart

Dear Salma Mam,
Whenever I think about the people who shaped me....Your name always comes first.. You did not just teach me lessons from book.. You taught me how to look at life differently... to trust myself.. and to keep moving forward even when things were difficult..
For me.. Teacher’s Day... is not just a date on the calender.... It is a reminder of how lucky I am to have you in my journey... The way you explain.. the way you guide.. and the way you believe in me... it feels less like teaching and more like lighting a flame that keeps me going.....
Sometimes I wonder.... If you were not there in my journey..Would I still have the same courage and clarity?.. Probably not... That's how big your impact is on me....
So Today... I don't just wish you a HAPPY Teacher’s DAY... I celebrate tou for being my mentor.. my guide.. and someone whose words live in my heart every single day...

With Heartfelt gratitude..
Maaz
